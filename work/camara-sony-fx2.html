Norway - Sony FX2

Type: Branding

Clients: Camara France & Sony

Steven and I headed to Norway’s Lofoten Islands to put the Sony FX2 through its paces. With jaw-dropping landscapes and ever-changing Arctic light, we wanted to see what this camera could truly deliver and it blew us away. From the Northern Lights to dramatic fjords, the FX2 captured every moment with stunning clarity and depth.
