05 — Key Rules
→Background is always Linen #F7F2EA, not pure white
→Headings in Cormorant Garamond Light or Italic
→All UI / nav text in Jost Light (300) spaced
→Terracotta only as an accent — never as a background
→Borders always 0.5px — never thick or harsh
→Image grid: 0px radius hero, 3px radius thumbs
→No uppercase for headings — only for micro-labels
→Generous whitespace is intentional, not empty
→Hover states use Gold Tint or Terracotta underline
→Aegean blue for subtle UI states and highlights
